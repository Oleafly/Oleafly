\begin{abstract}
This is a clean, single-column article template with sensible typography, ready
for a working paper, preprint, or course report. It uses only base LaTeX packages,
so it compiles anywhere and produces selectable, well-structured text. Replace this
abstract with a short summary of your contribution, results, and why they matter.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
State the problem, why it is interesting, and what you contribute. Cite related
work as you go~\cite{knuth1984}. Keep paragraphs focused and lead with the point.

\section{Method}
Describe your approach. Inline math like $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$ and display math both
work:
\begin{equation}
  \mathcal{L}(\theta) = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n} \ell\big(f_\theta(x_i), y_i\big).
  \label{eq:loss}
\end{equation}
Reference equations, figures, and tables by label, for example Eq.~\eqref{eq:loss}
and Table~\ref{tab:results}.

\section{Results}
Summarize what you found. A simple table:
